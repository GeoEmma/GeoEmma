    Research Interests

    Climate and land use land cover change (LULCC) are expected to greatly impact the futuredistributions of species, particularly in tropical regions. My current research focusses on how to couple the latest in species distribution and spatial predictive modelling to improveour understanding of how biodiversity patterns in tropical regions will be affected by the synergistic effects of multiple global change drivers, including climatic, land-use and landcover change.
